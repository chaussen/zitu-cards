Print

A4 stock at 100% scale, 120 gsm or heavier. 米字格 grids print at hairline weight so they don't compete with the character.

贰

Duplex

Two-sided · flip on long edge. Back sheets are pre-mirrored so each blueprint lines up with its front.

叁

Cut & sort

Trim along borders. Cards finish at 100×142mm. Stack by tone, by formation type, or by HSK level — the system supports all three.

肆

Use

Quiz with the front. Study with the back. Drill on the practice sheet. Display the poster. One pack a week.
